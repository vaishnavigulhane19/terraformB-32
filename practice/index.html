About
the Studio

Compression is a design practice built on the belief that the best work happens under constraints. We take complex problems and compress them into clear, decisive systems.

Founded in 2020, the studio operates at the intersection of architecture, digital product, and visual identity. Our process is reductive — we strip away until only the essential remains.
